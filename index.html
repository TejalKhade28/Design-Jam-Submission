Our Special Donuts

Strawberry Frosted Donuts

Soft, cakey, and moist homemade donuts are baked not fried, and topped with strawberry frosting!

This Strawberry Frosted Donut recipe includes a flavorful fresh strawberry icing and ideas to substitute for a plain glaze, in a pinch. Make these pretty little glamour donuts for Valentine’s Day, or anytime you want to show your love to your closest friends and family.

Jelly Donuts

Donuts that are delicious any time of the year, but classically made and eaten during Hanukkah(a jewish festival)!

These jelly doughnuts are soft, airy and fluffy and filled with delicious strawberry jelly. These donuts also known as Sufganiyot – Jewish style donuts that usually made in Hanukkah. You can fill the donuts with any kind of jelly/jam; strawberry, blueberry, grape etc. You also can fill them with Nutella, pastry cream, dolce de leche and more.

BlueBerry Donuts

Classic Twist! Light and fluffy, and are super easy to whip up.

These blueberry baked donuts boast a very rich and soft brown butter base (my favorite!) that’s topped with fresh blueberry glaze. The glaze is just the touch of sweetness . They are truly delicious and you need to try them

Chocolate Frosted Donuts

A perfect dough in texture and a delicious chocolate glaze, made with real chocolate.

Chocolate frosted donuts are baked, not fried, and topped with a thick chocolate glaze. This easy recipe for moist and buttery donuts will quickly become a favorite breakfast treat. Cover them completely in sprinkles and you’ll find yourself eating more than just one!

Boston Creme Pie Donuts

The Perfect Desert-Fluffy donut is filled with a rich, creamy custard and topped with chocolate glaze

The Boston cream (pie) doughnut or donut is a round, solid, yeast-risen doughnut with chocolate frosting and a custard filling, resulting in a miniature doughnut version of the Boston cream pie.Despite their New England-centric name, Boston cream donuts are beloved worldwide.
